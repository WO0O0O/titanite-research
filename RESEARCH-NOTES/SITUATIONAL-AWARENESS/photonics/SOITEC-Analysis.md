1.  **Thesis Killer:** A delay in the commercial adoption of 1.6T silicon photonics transceivers. If hyperscalers continue to rely on traditional indium phosphide pluggable optics at 800G, Soitec's photonics volume ramp will defer to 2028, leaving the company exposed to the slow mobile phone recovery.
2.  **Short Report Reconciliation:** No active short seller reports exist.
3.  **Substitute Threat:** Foundries could standardise on Silicon Nitride substrates for waveguides. While Silicon Nitride offers low loss, it has a lower refractive index contrast than SOI, making the resulting PICs significantly larger and more difficult to integrate, preserving Soitec's advantage.
4.  **Concentration Stress Test:** If Soitec's largest customer (estimated at 20% of revenue) cancelled its contracts, annual revenue would fall by €120 million. Underutilisation charges would expand, compressing gross margins back to single digits and delaying the path to profitability.
5.  **Technology Skip Risk:** The development of distributed GPU training across multiple distinct locations using standard telecom optical networks would reduce the density of intra-cluster connections. However, this technology remains speculative and does not resolve latency constraints.
6.  **Capital Structure Failure:** If capital markets locked up, Soitec would have to fund capex from operating cash flow. This would require halting the Singapore expansion, delaying the SmartSiC volume ramp.
7.  **Geopolitical Disruption:** If a blockade of Taiwan occurred, TSMC's manufacturing would stop, halting Soitec's primary downstream channel. However, Soitec's European and Singaporean manufacturing sites would remain operational, allowing it to supply Western-based fabless customers.
8.  **Government Nationalisation:** Under a US/European nationalisation scenario, Soitec's French Bernin facilities would be designated as strategic assets. The company would receive sovereign funding and guaranteed cost-plus contracts to supply domestic foundries, protecting its revenue floor.
