# IQE plc (IQE) Research Notes

**Date:** 31 May 2026  
**Subject:** Update on FY 2025 Financial Results, Capital Restructure, and MACOM Strategic Investment

---

## 1. FY 2025 Financial Results (28 May 2026)

*   **Financial Performance:**
    *   **Revenue:** Recorded at £97.3 million, down 17.6% YoY compared to FY 2024 (£118.0 million). The decline reflects underutilisation of manufacturing assets and cyclical mobile handset destocking.
    *   **Adjusted EBITDA:** Decreased 60% YoY to £3.2 million (FY 2024: £8.1 million), with EBITDA margin dropping to 3.3%.
    *   **Loss Before Tax:** £37.0 million (FY 2024: loss of £36.9 million).
*   **Segment Dynamic:**
    *   **Photonics:** Revenue grew 15% YoY to £57.1 million, driven by demand for Indium Phosphide (InP) and Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) solutions in AI and data-center optical networks, as well as US military/defence programmes.
    *   **Wireless:** Revenue fell 40% YoY to £40.1 million, reflecting cyclical inventory adjustments in the smartphone supply chain.
*   **FY 2026 Guidance:** Management guided for revenue growth exceeding 20% year-on-year for FY 2026, driven by a strong Q1 order book and H2 visibility, with adjusted EBITDA margins inflecting to a high-single digit to low double-digit level.

---

## 2. Capital Restructure & MACOM Partnership (28 May 2026)

*   **Fundraising Details:** IQE successfully completed its strategic fundraising, raising £81 million in gross proceeds. 
    *   **HSBC Debt Repayment:** The proceeds were used to repay the revolving credit facility (RCF) with HSBC and clear historical loan notes. This restructure removes restrictive debt covenants that had constrained the balance sheet.
*   **MACOM Strategic Investment:**
    *   **Investment Details:** MACOM Technology Solutions Inc. invested £45 million, consisting of £30 million in equity (151,515,151 new shares at 19.8 pence per share) and £15 million in zero-coupon convertible loan notes.
    *   **Ownership Stake:** Post-transaction, MACOM holds an 11.5% voting stake in IQE.
    *   **Board Representation:** Two MACOM executives—Robert Dennehy (COO) and David O'Carroll (VP)—are appointed to the IQE Board of Directors as non-executive directors.
    *   **Commercial Agreements:** Concurrent Long-Term Supply Agreements (LTSAs) for advanced epitaxial services were signed, binding MACOM's optical transceiver material needs to IQE.
