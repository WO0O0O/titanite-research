## SECTION 1 — WHICH AI INFRA BOTTLENECK DOES IT SOLVE?
AMPG addresses the power/bandwidth bottleneck at the telecom edge via disaggregated AI-RAN networks and the thermal/readout bottleneck in quantum computing. High-density edge-AI workloads require low-latency wireless communication, which is throttled by traditional RAN architectures. AmpliTech's GPU-accelerated Massive MIMO radio systems optimize wireless performance and energy efficiency using AI-driven processing. Furthermore, superconducting quantum computers require extremely weak qubit signals to be amplified without introducing thermal noise. AmpliTech's 4 Kelvin (4K) cryogenic LNAs solve this readout bottleneck, permitting high-fidelity signal amplification near absolute zero.
